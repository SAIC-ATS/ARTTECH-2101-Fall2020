ATS Practices

Fall 2020 - SAIC Art and Technology Studies

This team-taught, introductory course provides a foundation for most additional coursework in the ATS department. Students are given a broad interdisciplinary grounding in the skills, concepts, and hands-on experiences they will need to engage the potentials of new technologies in art making. Every other week, a lecture and discussion group exposes students to concepts of electronic media, perception, inter-media composition, emerging venues, and other issues important to artists working with technologically based media. Students gain hands-on experience with a variety of forms and techniques central to technologically-based art making.
